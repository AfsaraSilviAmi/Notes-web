✔️for of

for of is used in arrays. The arrays are then serially printed as singular numbers and not array.

let numbers=[4,88,12];
for(const num of numbers){
console.log(num);
}

✔️while loop

In while loop, at first we initialize then inside ( ) we write the condition and finally the incrimination/decrimination in written inside the loop.

✔️for loop

In for loop, initialize, condition and incrimination/decrimination are in written inside the ( ).

✔️break

Break is used to STOP the loop completely according to conditions.

✅For square root ---> Number.isInteger(Math.sqrt()) is needed.

✔️continue

Continue is used to SKIP the particular element according to the conditions.
